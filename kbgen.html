Technicality Tiers

One recording.
Every audience.

The same workflow becomes a different article depending on who needs to read it. KBGen adjusts the language, detail level, and screenshot density to match your audience.

Tier 0

End Users

Every click gets a screenshot. Big red arrows, boxes, and circles highlight exactly where to look. Plain language, zero jargon, zero assumptions. Written so anyone can follow along.

Tier 1

Help Desk / Power Users

Assumes basic familiarity with the application. Fewer screenshots, focused on the steps that aren't obvious. Includes context without over-explaining every click.

Tier 2

Desktop Services

Written for teams that configure and maintain applications. More technical language, screenshots only where they add real value, and enough detail to execute without hand-holding.

Tier 3

Subject Matter Experts

Concise, direct instructions for people who know the system. Proper technical terminology, minimal screenshots, and no unnecessary explanation. Just what they need to get it done.

Talk through what you're doing while you record. Your narration becomes the written instructions. KBGen translates your explanation into clean, structured steps.
